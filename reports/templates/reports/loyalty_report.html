Loyalty Program Report

About the Loyalty Program

The Loyalty Program report identifies valuable clients who have made significant purchases in the previous month. These are clients with total purchases exceeding 5,000,000 COP during the previous calendar month.

This report provides a list of all loyalty-eligible clients along with their contact information and total purchases amount. It helps our company identify and reward our most valuable customers.

The report generates an Excel file containing data from the previous month. It can be used for marketing campaigns, special promotions, and customer relationship management.
